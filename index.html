The Genesis

Founded with a singular vision, Q Tv Media Telugu was born out of deep necessity. We recognized that the Telugu audience was subjected to theatrical debates and partisan agendas, starving for raw, unfiltered reality.

We engineered a structural solution. By leveraging hyper-localized digital journalism and cloud-based algorithmic distribution, we established a network that reports live from the streets. Our editorial board strictly enforces neutral data analysis. Speed is our weapon; truth is our absolute shield.
